# Future Features Recommendations

Here is a backlog of suggested features to implement in the future to elevate the Library Management project:

### 🌟 Community & Reader Ecosystem
1. **Book Reviews & Ratings:**
   - Allow users to rate (star rating) and review books they've read.
   - Display Average Rating on the Catalog page to increase trust and discoverability.
2. **Reading Lists / Collections:**
   - Add a "Bookmark" or "Save to List" feature.
   - Allow users to create custom lists such as *To-Read*, *Favorites*, or *Research Material*.
3. **Gamification & Challenges:**
   - Award Badges or points when users read a certain number of books per month.
   - Introduce reading streaks and community leaderboards.

### 🚀 Digital Experience Upgrades
4. **Embedded E-Reader / Audio Player:**
   - Instead of just providing download links for documents, integrate a Web PDF Viewer (e.g., PDF.js) or Audio Player.
   - Users can read or listen to books directly within the web app with a minimalist interface (similar to Kindle Web).
5. **AI-Powered Recommendations:**
   - Suggest books based on the user's borrowing history or study room booking history.
   - Provide "Because you read X, you might like Y" sections on the home page.

### 💳 Admin & Payments Enhancement
6. **Online Fines Payment Gateway:**
   - Integrate Stripe or PayPal API so students/readers can pay their overdue fines online, rather than needing to pay in cash at the librarian's desk.
7. **QR Code Check-in / Checkout:**
   - Generate unique QR codes for physical books and study rooms.
   - Users can scan QR codes with their phones to instantly checkout a book or check-in to a reserved study room.
